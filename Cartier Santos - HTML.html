Cartier

Santos de Cartier

Intro

The Cartier Santos is one of the most enduring and influential wristwatches in history. Originally designed in 1904 for aviator Alberto Santos-Dumont, it holds the distinction of being the first wristwatch for men, blending form with function in an era dominated by pocket watches.

Over the years, the Santos has been reimagined while retaining its distinctive square case, visible screws, and elegant design. In this review, we delve into why the Cartier Santos is a symbol of sophistication and innovation in the world of luxury timepieces.

Design

The Cartier Santos stands out with its square case, a departure from traditional round watches. The visible screws on the bezel are a bold design choice, lending the watch an industrial yet refined aesthetic. With options for steel, gold, or two-tone cases, the Santos offers versatility for every style.

The iconic dial features Roman numerals and sword-shaped hands, combining classic and modern elements seamlessly. The bracelet incorporates Cartier's innovative QuickSwitch and SmartLink systems, making it easy to change straps or adjust the size without tools.

Specifications:

Weight 135g (varies depending on model)
Case Diameter Available in 35mm (small), 39mm (medium), 43mm (large)
Case Thickness 9.08mm (medium)
Band Width 19mm (medium), 21mm (large)
Material Stainless Steel, Gold, Two-tone
Crystal Sapphire
Water Resistance 100 meters (excluding pre-2010 models)

Movement:

Caliber number 1847 MC
Power Reserve Approximately 42 hours
Frequency/Vibrations per hour 28,800 vibrations per hour (4Hz)
Accuracy -3/+7 seconds per day
Functions Hours, minutes, seconds, date
Special Features Anti-magnetic nickel-phosphorus components

The 1847 MC movement ensures precise timekeeping and reliability, with added resistance to magnetic fields, making it suitable for modern lifestyles.

Different Variants (Check pictures on right)

  • Santos de Cartier: The classic model with steel or gold options.
  • Santos Dumont: A slimmer, more refined version, ideal for formal occasions.
  • Santos Chronograph: A sportier take with chronograph functionality.

Each model offers customizable straps in leather or metal, catering to both traditional and contemporary preferences.

Dials

The dials of the Cartier Santos are designed with clarity and elegance in mind. The Roman numerals and minute track exude timeless sophistication, while the blue steel hands and sapphire cabochon crown add a touch of Cartier's signature style.
