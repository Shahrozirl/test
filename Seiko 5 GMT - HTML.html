Seiko

Seiko 5 GMT Series

Intro

The Seiko 5 GMT collection is an exciting addition to Seiko's well-known 5 series, offering a practical yet stylish watch for those who need more than just the time.

Designed with the modern traveler in mind, these watches feature a GMT function, allowing you to track a second time zone with ease—ideal for frequent flyers or anyone who needs to stay connected across different parts of the world.

Known for its design and affordability, the Seiko 5 line has been a great pick for entry-level watch enthusiasts, and the GMT models build on that legacy with enhanced functionality. The collection offers a range of designs but still quite simple, but that is how Seiko runs.

Whether you specifically need to view multiple timezones or are simply looking for a beater watch, then the Seiko 5 GMT line provides a perfect balance of performance, value, and style.

Design

We have had multiple homages or even direct copies of more eye-catching watches and Seiko is'nt a stranger to this, however they have had a refreshing new design for the new GMT line. Notably the Batman Blueberry colorway can be seen asa homage to the Rolex GMT Master II.

Instead of the matt/glossy ceramic bezel most watches would have, Seiko has instead opted for a glass feel with a high polishing.

The PD-1662 GMT Seiko NH34 offers great readability in all circumstances, especially in the dark. Its large hands, indexes and bezel numerals are coated with a long-lasting luminescent material.

The movement itself is displayed for people to see due to the open case back, the movement has 24 jewels that are nice to look at. Function: 4 Hands including a 2nd hour hand for GMT function, automatic and manual winding, date function.

Different Variants (Check pictures on right)

There are a multitude of variants however the main four colorways are Black Blue (Batman), Black Green (Starbucks), Blue Red (Pepsi) and Rose Gold (Rootbeer). There are different straps available for all the watches, you can choose between an oyster strap, jubilee strap and rubber strap.

Seiko 5 Sports has launched a limited edition watch in partnership with top Japanese skateboarder Yuto Horigome.

This is the first SKX GMT series watch to feature a black-coated stainless steel case and bracelet.

The bezel's navy and purple tones are inspired by Tokyo's downtown sunsets, where Horigome honed his skateboarding skills. The back of the watch displays Horigome's signature (Please see image on top-right) , along with “LIMITED EDITION” and a unique serial number, marking its exclusivity.

Dials (Check pictures on right)

The dials range from a multitude of colors which I really enjoy, usually there is'nt enough choice but Seiko has outdone themselves with the design and I'm happy with the current selection.

Final Verdict

My final verdict of the watch after owning it for multiple years is that for the price this watch blows everything out of the water.

I'm overall very pleased with the Pagani Design PD-1662. For the price, it offers impressive quality and a solid range of features, making it a great value.

It's both attractive and practical, fully meeting my need for a GMT watch. While it's certainly not a Rolex— and doesn't pretend to be—it's a worthwhile alternative for those who want a GMT watch without a sky-high price tag.

I'd recommend this watch to anyone who appreciates the design and functionality of the Rolex GMT Master II but is open to a more affordable option with a few compromises, such as potential movement quirks and a more mainstream look.
