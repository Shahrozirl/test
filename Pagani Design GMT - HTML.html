Pagani Design

PD-1662 GMT Seiko NH34

Intro

If you're a fan of high-quality timepieces but not so much for luxury brands or prices, then Pagani Design is the brand for you and this watch may just be the perfect addition to your collection.

Known for delivering impressive design and functionality at a fraction of the cost of high-end Swiss brands, Pagani Design has earned a reputation among watch enthusiasts for its value-packed watches.

In this review, we'll dive into the details of the Pagani Design GMT (Batman colorway), exploring its build quality, performance, design features, and, of course, how it stacks up against its pricier competitors. Whether you're a seasoned watch collector or just beginning to explore the world of watches, this piece promises to impress.

Design

The watch is a homage of the famous Rolex GMT Master II. The desing is very well made and it is comfortable to wear. The bezel is ceramic which is very good for the price.

However, The watch is not without flaws, my biggest complain is'nt the desing but the functionality of the clasp. It is very tight and it was difficult to close the clasp over your wrist.

Movement:

Frequency 21600 bph
Accuracy -20~+40 sec per day
Function Automatic and manual winding, date function
Jewels 24 jewels

The PD-1662 GMT Seiko NH34 offers great readability in all circumstances, especially in the dark. Its large hands, indexes and bezel numerals are coated with a long-lasting luminescent material.

Different Variants

There are a multitude of variants however the main four colorways are Black Blue (Batman), Black Green (Starbucks), Blue Red (Pepsi) and Rose Gold (Rootbeer). There are different straps available for all the watches, you can choose between an oyster strap, jubilee strap and rubber strap.

Dials

All dials are a black colour which helps bring out the beauty of the bezels. Pagani Design also has a decent lume which can glow in the dark for a few hours but I would like to see some better lume in future models. (Please see image on right)

Final Verdict

My final verdict of the watch after owning it for multiple years is that for the price this watch blows everything out of the water.

I'm overall very pleased with the Pagani Design PD-1662. For the price, it offers impressive quality and a solid range of features, making it a great value.

It's both attractive and practical, fully meeting my need for a GMT watch. While it's certainly not a Rolex—and doesn't pretend to be—it's a worthwhile alternative for those who want a GMT watch without a sky-high price tag.

I'd recommend this watch to anyone who appreciates the design and functionality of the Rolex GMT Master II but is open to a more affordable option with a few compromises, such as potential movement quirks and a more mainstream look.
